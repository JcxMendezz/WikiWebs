Wikis as a source of monetization

Wikis are collaborative websites that allow users to create content together. Although most wikis are created for the purpose of sharing information for free, there are several ways to monetize a wiki or use it as part of a revenue-generating strategy.

Premium subscriptions on your sites

providing benefits to your most loyal customers creates a source of income to your wiki

Offering premium memberships or paid subscriptions is one way to monetize a wiki. You can provide exclusive content or benefits to members who pay a monthly or annual fee. Also allowing visitors to make voluntary donations to maintain the wiki is an option. Some wikis use platforms such as Patreon or PayPal to receive donations from their followers.
